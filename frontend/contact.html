Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will you respond?

We aim to respond to all email queries within 4 working hours. Phone support is available Mon–Sat, 9 AM to 7 PM.

Is the first counselling session really free?

Yes! Your first 30-minute session with any of our counsellors is completely free — no credit card needed.

How do I report a technical issue?

Select "Technical Issue" in the subject dropdown and describe the problem. Include your browser and OS for faster resolution.

Can I collaborate with EduPortal?
