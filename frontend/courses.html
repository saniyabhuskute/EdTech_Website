Career Paths After Engineering

The wide range of opportunities available with an engineering degree in today's job market.

🏢

Core Engineering Jobs

ISRO, DRDO, L&T, Tata Motors, Infosys hardware division — packages ₹4–12 LPA.

💻

Software Industry

TCS, Infosys, Google, Microsoft, Amazon — packages ₹8–60+ LPA for CSE/IT/AI branches.

🎓

Higher Studies (M.Tech / MS)

GATE, GRE — pursue M.Tech at IITs or MS/PhD abroad at top global universities.

📈

MBA After B.Tech

IIMs, XLRI, IIFT — tech + management combo is highly valued. Packages ₹20–60 LPA.

Government/PSU

UPSC ESE, GATE-based PSU recruitment (BHEL, NTPC, ONGC) — stable careers with ₹8–15 LPA.
