Step-by-Step Process

Engineering Admission Process
Full Roadmap

Follow these 8 steps to ensure a smooth admission process for B.Tech / BE programmes.

1
Choose Your Entrance Exam

JEE Main (NITs, IIITs, CFTIs), JEE Advanced (IITs), MHT-CET (Maharashtra), BITSAT (BITS), VITEEE (VIT) etc.

💡 Most students appear for JEE Main + one state-level exam as backup.
2
Register & Prepare

Create accounts on NTA, state CET portals. Upload documents, pay fees and start your preparation.

3
Appear for Exam

Take the exam at your assigned centre. JEE Main has two sessions — attempt both for the best percentile.

4
Check Results & Rank

Results are published on NTA's official website. Check your percentile, rank and qualifying status.

5
JoSAA / CSAB Registration

Register on the counselling portal. Keep your rank card, Aadhaar and Class 12 marksheet ready.

💡 Use EduPortal's cutoff tool to shortlist realistic colleges before choice filling begins.
6
Choice Filling

Add colleges and branches in order of preference. Be strategic — add 50+ choices to maximise your chances.

7
Seat Allotment & Payment

If allotted, pay the acceptance fee online. Choose Float, Freeze or Slide based on your preference.

8
Document Verification & Reporting

Visit the allotted college with all original documents. Complete physical verification and admission formalities.

Documents Checklist

Required Documents for Admission

📋

Class 10 Marksheet & Certificate

Original + 3 photocopies. Used for DOB verification.

📋

Class 12 Marksheet & Certificate

Original + 5 photocopies. Must show Physics, Chemistry & Maths.

🎫

JEE Main / Advanced Admit Card

Print multiple copies. Required for all verification steps.

📊

JEE Scorecard / Rank Card

Download from NTA website. Keep both digital and print copies.

📷

Passport-Size Photos

10 recent passport photos (white background). Same as used in application.

🆔

Aadhaar Card

Required for identity and address proof during verification.
