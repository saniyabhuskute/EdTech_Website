Engineering Admission Process
Full Roadmap

Follow these 8 steps to ensure a smooth admission process for B.Tech / BE programmes.

1
Appear in JEE Main (Jan & April Sessions)

JEE Main is conducted by NTA twice a year. Your best percentile from both attempts is considered. Register on jeemain.nta.nic.in using Aadhaar card.

💡 Pro tip: Attempt both sessions. Many students improve their percentile by 5–10% in the second attempt.
2
Check JEE Main Result & Qualify for JEE Advanced

Top 2.5 lakh JEE Main qualifiers are eligible for JEE Advanced. Results declare on NTA website. Download scorecard and check your raw score, percentile and rank.

💡 For NITs, GFTIs — JEE Main rank alone is sufficient. JEE Advanced is only for IITs.
3
Register for JoSAA Counselling

Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) conducts centralised counselling for IITs, NITs, IIITs and GFTIs. Register on josaa.nic.in with your JEE rank and fill college-branch preferences in order of priority.

💡 Fill 30–50 options minimum. More options = better chance of getting a seat. Always add safe options at the end.
4
Choice Filling & Locking

JoSAA provides multiple windows (typically 5 rounds) for seat allotment. After filling choices, review and lock them. You can edit choices before each round closes.

💡 Research opening and closing ranks of previous 5 years for each college-branch before filling choices.
5
Seat Allotment & Document Verification

After each allotment round, visit the nearest reporting centre or do online document verification. Pay the partial acceptance fee (₹35,000 for general / ₹15,000 for SC/ST) to keep the seat.

💡 Keep digital copies of all documents ready. Original documents needed for physical verification.
6
Float / Freeze / Slide Decision

If you get a seat but want a better one, choose Float (upgrade attempts continue). Choose Freeze if satisfied with current allotment. Slide allows upgrade within same institute.

💡 If you get your target college or a better option in later rounds, float. If rank is near the cutoff, consider freezing.
7
Pay Full Seat Acceptance Fee

After final allotment, pay the remaining seat acceptance fee online. Keep the payment receipt safe — it is required during college reporting.

8
Report to Allotted College

Report to your allotted college on the specified date with all original documents, photographs, fees demand draft and printed JoSAA seat acceptance letter. Complete medical examination at the college.

💡 Carry 10–15 photocopies of each document. Wear formal or smart casual for reporting day.
Documents Checklist

Required Documents for Admission

Have all these documents ready before reporting to your college. Missing documents can delay/cancel admission.

📋

Class 10 Marksheet & Certificate

Original + 3 photocopies. Used for DOB verification.

📋

Class 12 Marksheet & Certificate

Original + 5 photocopies. Must show Physics, Chemistry & Maths.

🎫

JEE Main / Advanced Admit Card

Print multiple copies. Required for all verification steps.

🏆

JEE Rank Card / Scorecard

Download from official NTA website and print.

🆔

Aadhaar Card

Original + 2 photocopies. Mandatory for identity verification.

📸

Recent Passport Photos

10–15 recent passport-size photographs (white/light background).

📄

Category Certificate (if applicable)

SC/ST/OBC-NCL/EWS certificate issued by competent authority. Must be current year for OBC/EWS.

🏡

Domicile / Residence Proof

For state quota seats. Voter ID / Aadhaar / Ration card.

💊

Medical Fitness Certificate

From a registered MBBS doctor. Some institutes have specific formats.

🏦

Bank Account Details

For scholarship disbursement and refund processing.

💌

Migration Certificate

From your Class 12 board. May take 2–4 weeks — apply early!

🖊️

Transfer Certificate (TC)

From your Class 12 school/college. Ask for it immediately after results.
