We believe every student deserves access to clear, honest and complete information about colleges and careers.

Our Story

From IIT Grads to EdTech Pioneers

Founded in 2018 by a team of IIT and IIM alumni, EduPortal started as a simple cutoff comparison tool. Frustrated by scattered, outdated information, we set out to build the single most reliable source of college data in India.

Today, EduPortal helps over 2 million students every year discover colleges, understand admissions, and make confident career choices.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is EduPortal free to use?

Yes! Browsing colleges, comparing cutoffs, and reading our guides is completely free. We offer optional paid counselling sessions for personalised guidance.

How accurate is the cutoff data?

Our cutoff data is sourced directly from JoSAA, CSAB, NTA and individual university websites. We cross-verify every data point before publishing.

Can I book a counselling session for free?

Your first 15-minute introductory session is always free. Premium sessions are available at affordable rates starting from ₹499.

How many colleges does EduPortal cover?

We cover 10,000+ colleges across engineering, medical, management, law, design and other streams. New colleges are added regularly.
