Our Story

From a Confusion to a Revolution

In 2018, our founder Aryansh Mehta was a JEE aspirant who struggled to find reliable, centralised information about colleges, cutoffs and admission processes. Frustrated by scattered information across the internet, he decided to build the platform he wished had existed.

Today, EduPortal serves over 2 million students annually, with a dedicated team of 50+ education experts, counsellors and engineers working to democratise quality education guidance across India.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

🎯
Our Mission

To provide every student in India with accurate, unbiased and comprehensive information on colleges, courses and admissions — completely free of charge — so they can make informed decisions that shape their futures.

🔭
Our Vision

A future where no student misses their dream career due to lack of information or guidance. We envision EduPortal as the most trusted education intelligence platform in South Asia.

💎
Our Values

Transparency in all information, student-first approach in every decision, continuous innovation in technology, and genuine care for student outcomes form the core of everything we do.
